Our 8F1T experts can help you discover new healthy, convenient diets that offer a dynamic and efficient full-body fat loss.

Keto Diet


The ketogenic diet is a very low carb, high fat diet. This aims to force your body into using a different type of fuel - fat molecules.

Vegan Diet


Vegan diet involves eating only foods comprising plants. Those who follow this diet avoid all animal products, including meat and diary.

Intermittent Diet


The 5:2 diet is an intermittent fasting regime in which calorie intake is limited to 500–600 calories per day two times per week.

Extreme Diet


Extreme dieting involves reducing the calorie intake to lose lots of weight in a short period, pushing the body beyond its capabilities.
